March 2018 - Rocker Removal & Replacement (35 Minute Video)

BMW friends encouraged me to chronicle my next rocker repair in one of my “world famous” YouTube videos.  So, here it is!  35 minutes to demo the bad passenger side rockers and fabricate replacement new units.  What took me 2 – 3 months the previous fall, I was able to do basically do in 1 month because I knew what I was doing this go around.  This 35 minutes actually captures 80 – 100 hours of sweat and toil in the garage (aspiring amateur hours).
